EASY STEPS FOR THE USERS


    STEP 1. Click on farmer, you will get a form in which you have to fill in the details regarding farmer like farmer id, farmer name, location, crop, phone number, and quantity.
    STEP 2. Recheck the details whether filled correctly or not, then click on "Submit Details" button.
    STEP 3. Click on "Quality Testing", here when farmer id is entered we will get the details of the farmer and then the block number will be generated.
    STEP 4. Once we click on approve details button it will redirect to a form where the quality details of the crop should be entered, such as- test date, expiry date, grade, MRP, etc
    STEP 5. After filling in all the details click on "Submit" button.
    STEP 6. Next is the customer page, here we can get all the details of the farmer and the quality of the crops grown by him, by entering farmer's id and his lot number.
